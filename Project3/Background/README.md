# BIOS6624
This project provides:
	• a survival analysis to identify important baseline risk factors for stroke and
	• calculation of the 10-year probability of stroke for different risk profiles, stratified by sex, among participants who were free of prevalent stroke at the baseline examination.
	• evaluation on the degree of change in the diabetes status and blood pressure risk factors over time to determine if a more complex time-varying covariate analysis would be needed in future research.
